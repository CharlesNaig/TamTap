Introduction

TAMTAP is committed to protecting the fundamental human rights of privacy on personal information pursuant to the provisions of Republic Act No. 10173, also known as the Data Privacy Act of 2012, and its implementing Rules and Regulations and the Basic Education Act of 1982.

Administration, teachers, students, and other stakeholders including parents are enjoined to share in the responsibility to secure and protect personal and/or sensitive information collected and processed by TAMTAP in pursuit of legitimate purposes.

Information We Collect

TAMTAP collects the following information strictly for attendance monitoring:

  • Student identification: Full name, section, grade level, and TAMTAP ID
  • NFC card data: Unique card identifier linked to a registered student
  • Attendance timestamps: Date, time, and session (AM/PM) of each tap
  • Face detection photos: Photos captured during tap-in for verification (face detection only — no facial recognition or biometric matching is performed)

Storage and Retention

All information collected is stored using a locally hosted database and file system. The system operates entirely on the school's local area network (LAN) — no data is transmitted to external cloud services, third-party providers, or the public internet.

Data is retained only for the duration of the current school year (S.Y. 2025–2026) and will be archived or securely deleted at the end of the academic term unless otherwise required by school administration.

Sharing and Disclosure

The information collected is not shared with or sold to other organizations for commercial purposes except for the following circumstances:

  • When required by school administration for academic records
  • When it is necessary to share information to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding unauthorized system access or misuse
  • When otherwise required by Philippine law (R.A. 10173)

Your Data Rights

Under the Data Privacy Act of 2012, you have the following rights:

  • Right to be Informed: You have the right to know what data TAMTAP collects and how it is used
  • Right to Access: You may request access to your attendance records
  • Right to Correct: You may request correction of any inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: You may request deletion of your data, subject to retention requirements
  • Right to Object: You may object to the processing of your data under certain conditions

Security Measures

TAMTAP implements the following security measures to protect collected data:

  • All data is stored on a local Raspberry Pi server, isolated from the internet
  • Access to the dashboard requires authenticated login credentials
  • Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms (bcrypt)
  • Only authorized teachers and administrators can view attendance records
  • The system operates exclusively on the school LAN

Contact

For questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy or the handling of your data, please contact the school administration or the research team through the FEU Roosevelt Marikina campus.
